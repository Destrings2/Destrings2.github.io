Enhanced Software Developer Burnout Risk Assessment

This 40-item assessment evaluates your burnout risk across five key domains, providing targeted insights and evidence-based prevention strategies.

Instructions

Rate each statement from 1 to 5:

  • 1 = Strongly Disagree
  • 2 = Disagree
  • 3 = Neutral
  • 4 = Agree
  • 5 = Strongly Agree

Note: Some items are reverse-scored, meaning a lower score is better.

Assessment Domains

  • Personal Vulnerability Patterns: How your personal tendencies and psychological patterns influence your susceptibility to burnout.
  • Work Environment Factors: How your work environment, organization, and job structure impact your wellbeing.
  • Recovery Mechanisms: How effectively you disconnect from work and restore your mental and physical energy.
  • Support & Buffer Systems: The presence and quality of professional and personal support systems in your life.
  • Motivation & Alignment: How well your work aligns with your interests, values, and sense of meaning.

Privacy Note

All data is processed locally in your browser. No information is sent to any server or stored permanently.
